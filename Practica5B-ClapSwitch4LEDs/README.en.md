# Clap Switch

## Parts list

* Arduino Nano
* 4x LEDs
* "Big Sound" KY-038
* 8x Dupont male-male cables
* Breadboard

## Project goals

Turn on/off up to 4 LEDs with claps:
* 2 claps turn on/off LED 1
* 3 claps turn on/off LED 2
* 4 claps turn on/off LED 3
* 5 claps turn on/off LED 4
* 6 claps turn off all LEDs
